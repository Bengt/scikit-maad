util.decibelSPL¶

Collection of functions to convert audio and voltage data to Sound Pressure Level (SPL in Pascal) and Leq (Continuous Equivalent SPL).

Functions

PSD2Leq

convert Power spectral density (PSD, amplitude²) into Equivalent Continuous Sound pressure level (Leq [dB SPL])

amplitude2dBSPL

convert signal (amplitude) to instantaneous sound pressure level (L [dB SPL])

dBSPL2pressure

convert sound pressure level (L [dB]) to sound pressure (p [Pa])

power2dBSPL

convert power (amplitude²) to sound pressure level (L [dB])

pressure2Leq

convert pressure (p [Pa]) to Equivalent Continuous Sound Pressure level (Leq [dB SPL])

pressure2dBSPL

convert sound pressure (p [Pa]) to sound pressure level (L [dB])

volt2pressure

convert volt to instantaneous sound pressure (p [Pa])

wav2Leq

convert wave to Equivalent Continuous Sound Pressure level (Leq [dB SPL])

wav2dBSPL

convert wave to instantaneous sound pressure level (L [dB SPL])

wav2pressure

convert wave to instantaneous sound pressure (p [Pa])

wav2volt

convert in Volt
